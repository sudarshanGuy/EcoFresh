About Me!

Hi, I’m Sudarshan Rao, and I’m currently in Grade 10. I’ve always had a strong interest in coding, 3D printing, and computers. These fields fascinate me because they allow me to create and explore new possibilities. One of my most exciting projects is EcoFresh, an app I developed to address a significant global issue: energy consumption and its impact on the environment. I chose to create this product because I believe that energy efficiency is crucial, especially as we move toward more sustainable cities. The app helps people monitor and control their devices, reducing energy waste and lowering CO2 emissions. I’ve always been passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems, and EcoFresh is the perfect example of that. Aside from my work on EcoFresh, I also love experimenting with 3D printing, coding, and diving deeper into the world of computers. I’m constantly learning new skills, which helps me grow both as a student and as someone who wants to make a difference. With projects like EcoFresh, I hope to contribute to a better, more sustainable future. Outside of my tech interests, I also focus on enhancing my problem-solving skills, which I believe are essential in both academics and life. My goal is to keep pushing boundaries in tech and develop solutions that can create meaningful change.
